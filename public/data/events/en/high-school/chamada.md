#### Overview Geral

*CBSoft for High School* is an initiative linked to CBSoft - the largest software engineering conference in Brazil - aimed at bringing high school students closer to the field of Software Engineering and its career opportunities.

#### Objective

To foster interest among school-age youth in technology, particularly Software Engineering, by presenting the field, its professional possibilities, and its impact on the job market. The initiative encourages these students future entry into the sector.

#### Target Audience

High school students, especially from public schools.

#### Format of Activities

The event will be structured around interactive and inspiring activities, including:

* Introductory Lectures
 Presenting the field of Software Engineering, covering basic concepts, career opportunities, and an overview of the job market.

* Participation of Industry Professionals
 Guests will share their career paths, experiences, and challenges to connect with students and enhance their sense of belonging and representation in the field.

* Panel with Public School Alumni
 A space for dialogue with students and professionals who came from public schools, discussing their academic and professional journeys in Software Engineering, highlighting paths, challenges, and strategies for success.

#### Differentiators

* Direct connection between high school students and active industry professionals
* Appreciation of diverse career paths, promoting representation
* Integration with one of the leading scientific events in the field in Brazil
* Opportunity to explore a public university and its various possibilities through a visit to the main campus of the University of São Paulo
* Encouragement of interest in technology careers from basic education onwards

#### Expected Impact

* Increased interest among young people in the field of computing
* Greater awareness of opportunities in Software Engineering
* Encouragement to continue studies and pursue higher education
* Strengthening inclusion and diversity in the technology sector
